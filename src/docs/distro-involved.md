## Ways to Contribute

- **Test new builds** and report bugs or UI issues.
- **Suggest improvements** for workflows, panels, and wording.
- **Contribute code** to the frontend (React/Tauri) or backend helpers.
- **Help with documentation** for new users and power users.

## What We Appreciate

- Clear, reproducible bug reports.
- Thoughtful suggestions that consider different types of users.
- Small, focused merge requests that are easy to review.

## Getting Started

1. Run LinuxHub in a test environment (VM or spare machine).
2. Explore the configuration and packages sections.
3. Note anything confusing, broken, or missing.
4. Share your findings with the maintainers.

Your feedback directly shapes how LinuxHub grows and improves. Thank you for being involved.
